About Me

I'm a motivated professional with a strong background in Computer Science, pursuing a Master's degree while working as a Software Developer at Tyler Technologies. I strive to combine technical expertise with an understanding of human needs. As Frank Zappa once said, "The computer can give you the exact mathematical design, but what's missing is the eyebrows."
Throughout my academic journey, I've developed skills in organization, time management, and problem-solving through various roles. At Tyler Technologies, I effectively apply these skills, collaborating and prioritizing to contribute to the development process.
Beyond work, I have a passion for exploration and adventure, which fuels my creativity and problem-solving abilities when coding. I'm committed to personal and professional growth, actively seeking diverse collaborations to broaden my skill set.
As a dedicated, skilled, and compassionate professional in Software Development, I'm eager to connect with like-minded individuals making a meaningful impact through technology.
